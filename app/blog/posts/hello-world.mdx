# Welcome!

Hi there! I'm **Cun (Matthew) Mu** and this is my personal blog.

I'm a Director of Data Science at Walmart Labs, where I lead a team working on search ranking algorithms. My background spans Operations Research (Ph.D., Columbia University), optimization theory, and applied machine learning.

I'll be writing about topics I'm passionate about, including:

- **AI-powered search** — learning to rank, query understanding, and retrieval systems
- **Large Language Models** — practical applications in e-commerce and information retrieval
- **Machine learning** — optimization, tensor methods, and everything in between
- **Data science in industry** — lessons learned building ML systems at scale

## Why I Started This Blog

I've spent years reading blogs and papers that shaped how I think about problems. This is my way of giving back — sharing what I've learned in academia and industry, in a format that's accessible and practical.

## What to Expect

Posts will range from technical deep-dives to high-level discussions of trends in ML and search. I'll try to make the math clear and the code runnable wherever possible.
